The Story

GreenBelt UK started with a simple idea — what if everyone planted just one tree? Not as a corporate initiative. Not as a government programme. Just people. Planting trees. Registering them. Watching the count grow.

At its peak, GreenBelt UK had 449 registered trees across 12 countries, each one pinned on a live map so you could see the green spreading across the globe. Every tree registered added to the oxygen counter. Every school, every garden, every workplace that planted one became part of something bigger.

The site went quiet for a while. Life got in the way. But the trees didn't stop growing. And neither did the idea.

GreenBelt UK is coming back. When Sophia goes live, she'll be managing this alongside everything else — because building an empire means nothing if there's no world left to build it in.
